The Jewellery & Watches Edit: Everyday Luxury

Updated 2026 · 5 min read

There's a quiet kind of luxury that doesn't shout. It's the fine-gold chain you never take off, the watch that marks time through every important moment, the pair of earrings that finishes an outfit without trying. This is the everyday luxury edit — pieces worth investing in, all from stores that ship worldwide.

Start with one piece you'll wear every day

The most-worn jewellery is rarely the most expensive — it's the most versatile. A delicate gold necklace, a signet ring, a pair of small hoops: these go with everything and never date. Rather than a drawer full of trend pieces, build slowly around a few you genuinely love. Browse the full jewellery edit to find your first, or see what's popular right now:

The case for a proper watch

In a world of phone screens, a watch is a deliberate choice — a small daily pleasure and, chosen well, a piece that lasts decades. You don't need to spend a fortune; you need the right proportions, a strap you'll actually wear, and a movement you trust. Explore the watch collection, or start with these:

How to buy jewellery & watches online with confidence

  • Know your metals — solid gold and sterling silver last; plated pieces are fine for trends but wear over time. Check the description.
  • Mind the measurements — chain length, ring size and watch case diameter matter more than the photo suggests. Most listings state them.
  • Buy the classic, not the fad — a plain gold hoop outlives a novelty charm. Spend where you'll wear it for years.
  • Compare before you commit — the same style appears at very different prices across stores; we surface the best value.
