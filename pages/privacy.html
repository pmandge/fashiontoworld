Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — in plain, honest language.

Last updated: 31 May 2025

FashionToWorld ("we", "us", "our") operates the website fashiontoworld.co. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and what choices you have. We've tried to keep it readable rather than full of legal jargon.

Information We Collect

Information you give us

This is limited to what you choose to share — for example, your email address if you subscribe to our newsletter, or your name and message if you contact us. We do not ask for or store payment card details, because we never process payments ourselves.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, links clicked, and general interaction patterns, so we can improve the site.
  • Device & approximate location: your browser type and country (derived from your IP address) so we can show the right language, currency, and regional deals.
  • Cookies: small files that remember your preferences and help measure traffic. You can control these (see below).

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, personalise, and improve the website
  • To show relevant fashion deals in your language and currency
  • To send our newsletter, if you've subscribed (you can opt out anytime)
  • To respond to your messages and support requests
  • To measure the performance of links to our partner retailers

Affiliate Links & Tracking

When you click a product or deal, you're directed to a partner retailer through an affiliate network (such as Admitad). These networks use cookies to attribute any resulting purchase to us so we can earn a commission. This tracking does not capture your payment details. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more.

Cookies & Your Choices

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner, and you can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. Declining cookies won't stop you from browsing, though some preferences (like language) may not be remembered.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us run the site (for example, analytics and email delivery), and only as needed for them to perform those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here. If you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your data, we'll remove it promptly except where we must retain it by law.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR in the EU/UK), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@fashiontoworld.co.

Children

FashionToWorld is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll revise the "last updated" date above and, for significant changes, provide a more prominent notice.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@fashiontoworld.co.
