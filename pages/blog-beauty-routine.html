How to Build a Fragrance & Beauty Routine on a Budget

Updated 2026 · 5 min read

A good beauty routine isn't about owning the most products — it's about owning the right ones. The best part? Skincare, makeup and fragrance are some of the easiest things to buy internationally: small, light, and delivered almost anywhere. Here's how to build a routine you'll actually keep, without overspending.

Start with skincare — it does the heavy lifting

Great skin makes everything else easier. You don't need a ten-step routine; you need a gentle cleanser, one treatment that targets your concern, and a moisturiser you enjoy using. Build from there. Browse the full beauty edit, or start with what's popular now:

Find a signature scent (or two)

Fragrance is the most personal thing you'll wear — and one of the smartest things to buy online, since it ships easily worldwide. Rather than chasing every new launch, find one scent for day and one for evening, and let them become yours. A little goes a long way. Explore fragrance and more in the beauty section.

Makeup: a few good pieces beat a full drawer

The makeup that actually gets used is usually a short list — a base you trust, something for the eyes, a colour for the lips. Buy quality where it touches your skin, save on the rest. See what's trending:

How to shop beauty smart

  • Buy shelf-stable first — fragrance, tools and packaged skincare travel and last well.
  • Check sizes — a smaller bottle of something you love beats a big bottle you won't finish.
  • Read the ingredients — for skincare, the actives matter more than the branding.
  • Time your restock — beauty goes on sale too; the best deals are worth waiting for.
